Expenser is a personal expense tracking application designed to help users track their income and expenses efficiently. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Information We Collect

Expenser may collect the following types of information:

  • Financial Data: Expense and income data entered by the user, including transaction amounts, categories, dates, and notes
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage data (only if analytics is enabled), including app launches, feature usage, and crash reports
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (for analytics and crash reporting purposes only)

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address unless you voluntarily provide it through optional features (such as account creation or support requests).

Note: All financial data is stored locally on your device by default. If you choose to enable cloud backup or synchronization, your data may be stored on secure cloud servers.

How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used only to:

  • Provide expense tracking functionality
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and enhance features

Data Storage and Retention

Local Storage: All financial data is stored securely on your device by default. This data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

Cloud Storage: If you choose to enable cloud backup or synchronization, your data may be stored on secure cloud servers. You can disable this feature at any time.

Data Retention: We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account or request data deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with third parties for their marketing purposes. We only share data as described in the "Third-Party Services" section below.

Third-Party Services

Expenser uses the following third-party services to provide functionality and improve the app:

  • Google Firebase: We use Firebase for analytics and crash reporting. Firebase may collect device information, app usage data, and crash logs. You can learn more about how Firebase handles data by reviewing their privacy policy at: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing the collection and use of your information. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

Data Sharing: We only share data with these third-party services as necessary to provide app functionality. We do not share your financial transaction data with these services.

Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest (when using cloud services)
  • Secure authentication methods
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Expenser is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information from our servers.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can access all your data directly within the app
  • Correction: You can edit or correct your data at any time through the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual transactions or all your data at any time. To delete all data, you can uninstall the app or use the "Delete All Data" option in app settings. If you have a cloud account, you can delete your account through the app settings
  • Data Portability: You can export your data in various formats (CSV, JSON) through the app's export feature
  • Opt-Out: You can disable analytics and crash reporting in the app settings

To exercise any of these rights, please use the app's built-in features or contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

Account Deletion

If you have created an account with Expenser, you can delete your account at any time through the app settings. When you delete your account:

  • All your data associated with the account will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • You will no longer be able to access your account or any data associated with it
  • This action cannot be undone

If you need assistance with account deletion, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.
